construction Draft Phase

  • Buy items using your gold.
  • Select new talents when you level up.
  • Equip your items strategically.
  • Get a set bonus by equipping at least two items of the same set.
  • Once you're ready, face your opponent.

sports_martial_arts Battle Phase

  • The battle is fully automatic.
  • Fight against "ghosts" of other players.
  • Earn gold and experience after each battle.
  • You have three lives. Losing a battle costs one.
  • Your run ends when you run out of lives.
